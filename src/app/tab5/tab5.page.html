Welcome to the Outdoors

Beginning your journey toward becoming a hunter can be intimidating if you were not raised to do it. As a hunter, the goal is to harvest game as humanely as possible. This requires contious practice to ensure your skills and equipment are ready for the field.

It is important to understand that you are not expected to be an expert. We are all human, and mistakes will be made as part of the learning process. The best suggestion is to do your homework, follow the rules and regulations, practice, and most important, enjoy yourself.
