Hunt Doe During Rut

If you see a doe during rut, you are in luck. Buck are solely concerned with mating and are likely not far behind. Be patient and wait for your shot.

Make Noise

During rut, buck are often more responsive to calls. Do not be afraid to utilize grunts, bleats, or rattling antlers to get their attention.

Practice

Practice makes perfect. It is critical that you practice with your gear before you need to use it in the field. Proper training leads to successful hunts.
